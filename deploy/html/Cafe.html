When we conceptualized Vault + Vine, we wanted to create a space where both our guests and staff would feel at home. Located in the heart of East Falls, our cafe is the perfect place to unwind, meet a friend, or catch up on some work.

Stop by and enjoy our offerings from a variety of local vendors. Including coffee from Triangle Roasters: tea from the Random Tea Room and pastries from Au Fournil French Artisan Bakery, and Crust Vegan Bakery. The cafe is open from 8a-3p Thursday through Sunday.
